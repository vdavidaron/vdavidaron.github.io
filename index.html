Who we are

We are LUX Hostel, a hostel designed for students seeking affordable accommodation. Our mission is to provide comfortable and budget-friendly lodging options for students.

Short-term accommodation

Looking for a short-term stay? At LUX Hostel, we offer cozy and convenient lodging options that cater to your needs. Whether you're in town for a weekend getaway or a short study trip, we've got you covered.

Long-term accommodation

Planning to stay for an extended period? Our long-term accommodation options are perfect for students looking for a comfortable and affordable place to call home during their studies. With our convenient amenities and friendly atmosphere, you'll feel right at home at LUX Hostel.

Café

Craving a quick bite or a delicious cup of coffee? Visit our on-site cafe at LUX Hostel. Our cozy cafe offers a variety of snacks, beverages, and meals to satisfy your cravings. Whether you need a caffeine boost to start your day or a quick snack between classes, our cafe has something for everyone.

Local sights

Discover the beauty of our surroundings while staying at LUX Hostel. Located in a vibrant neighborhood, our hostel provides easy access to local sights and attractions. Whether you're interested in exploring historical landmarks, enjoying outdoor activities, or experiencing the local culture, there's something for everyone to enjoy nearby.

Bicycle rental

Explore the city on two wheels with our bicycle rental service. At LUX Hostel, we offer affordable bicycle rental options for students looking to explore the city at their own pace. Whether you're commuting to class or exploring local attractions, our bicycles are a convenient and eco-friendly way to get around.

And many more

At LUX Hostel, we strive to provide our guests with a comfortable and enjoyable stay. From our cozy accommodations to our convenient amenities, we aim to make your stay with us memorable. Whether you're here for a short visit or a long-term stay, we look forward to welcoming you to LUX Hostel.
